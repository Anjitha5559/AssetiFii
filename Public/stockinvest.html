Stock Marketing



What is Stock Marketing?



It is a public market where common people can buy and sell shares of public listed companies. Over time the value of your stock may increases or decrease with the growth and fall of the company. So, we have to choose the stock to be bought wisely. For Example, if you buy a stock of ‘X’ company for 200 rupees, then the company had a profit hike which will increase the stock say to 250 rupees. So, your profit till date will be 50 rupees. In India there are two Stock market indexes Sensex and Nifty50.



How to Earn?

Everybody invests in stock to increase their worth. If one wants a good profit, they have to understand the working of a stock market properly. Else it might lead to loss in your money.

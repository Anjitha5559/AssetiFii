2.Cryptocurrency Airdrop

Cryptocurrency users can get AIRDROP that is giving out of certain percentage of total supply of token to users. The criteria for these airdrops differs according to the project

  • Socialmedia airdrop

    • Users can get cryptocurrency token by doing some task on the social media.

  • Retroactive airdrop

    • Users can get the governance token of the project by the early users of that project. Projects like uniswap,dydx give 10000$ worth of assets to their early loyal users

  • Stakers airdrop

    • Users can get airdrop of other projects by staking other projects tokens Mostly the stakers of CosmosHub get the airdrop of other chains that are connected to CosmosHub.You can check the airdrops through this website You can buy cosmos on binance and then transfer to the keplr wallet and then you can stake.

Chegg

Chegg is a platform to ask doubts and learn. Students can earn through chegg by answering questions asked by others. For this, there are multiple exams to filter out the best candidate to be the experts. Before writing the exam, you have to link your PAN card. You will only get three attempts for taking the exam after which you can never attempt this exam with that PAN card. If you pass the exam you will get a mail containing the Guidelines and date by which your account will get activated. Once your account get activated you can answer for questions asked by others on your subject. Once you successfully complete a question by following their guidelines you will get paid for same after 15 of each month. If you have decent knowledge on a topic you can earn plenty by sparing very little time.
